Flex Heading

Accomplish

Horizontal or Vertical arrangement of elements

Space distribution of elements

Quick Notes

Flex is one-dimensional. Either affects a row or column but not both at same time.

Useful for layout on nav bars, headers, image galleries

Place elements that are direct children, but does not affect further children, like the ">" selector

Flex Container

Element with the 'display: flex' style. Style applies to container and children.

Justify Content

Position content along the axis of our flex container

  1. flex-start
  2. flex-end
  3. center
  4. space-between
  5. space-around
  6. space-evenly

Align Items

Similar to Justify Content but works along the cross-axis (perpendicular to the flex-direction)

  • flex-start
  • flex-end
  • center
  • stretch
  • baseline
